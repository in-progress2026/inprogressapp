Support — In Progress

In Progress is a local audio player for iOS. No subscriptions, no accounts, no internet required.

Common questions

How do I import files?

You can import audio files from the Files app, iCloud Drive, WhatsApp, or any app that supports iOS file sharing. You can also connect your iPhone to a Mac and drag files in via Finder.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. In Progress works completely offline, always.

What audio formats are supported?

In Progress supports the audio formats natively supported by iOS, including MP3, AAC, WAV, FLAC, and AIFF.
